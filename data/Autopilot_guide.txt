UiPath Autopilot is a new set of AI-powered experiences across the UiPath Platform that make every user from interns to CEOs more productive.
Autopilot for Developers: Build automation solutions in a breeze, create automations, apps, and expressions in UiPath Studio and Apps.
Developers can fluently express their design intentions textually or visually, leading to diverse and user-friendly interface designs. Furthermore, the opportunity to generate expressions across all apps ensures a unified and accelerated development process.

Autopilot for Testers: Empower all your testers to perform at their best with built-in, customizable AI. Accelerate every phase of the testing lifecycle with the power of an out-of-the-box agent.

Autopilot for Business Analysts: Unlock insights, and discover new automation opportunities at speed with UiPath Communications Mining and Process Mining.
Autopilot for Communications Mining gets your analytics and reporting questions answered at speed. Express queries in natural language and the correct filters are applied.
Autopilot for Process Mining making it easier for business analysts to easily filter, and create dashboards for discovering the highest-ROI optimization and automation opportunities.

Autopilot for Everyone: Complete everyday, time-consuming tasks in record time. Supercharge productivity with Autopilot for Everyone and UiPath Clipboard AI.
Autopilot for Everyone is an AI companion that helps with your daily tasks:
1. Works across your business apps and systems
2. Understands your work context and provides contextual guidance
3. Automates digital paperwork with best-in-class specialized AI models
4. Features interactive chat, powered by Gen AI
