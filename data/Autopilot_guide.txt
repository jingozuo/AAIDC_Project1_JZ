About this guide
This guide offers a comprehensive overview of the Autopilot initiative, covering its framework, tools, and benefits. It includes:
* Licensing options, including Unified Pricing and Flex licensing models.
* Data privacy considerations and best practices for secure usage.
* Best practices for effective interaction with Autopilot, including writing prompts and designing relevant automations.
* Detailed instructions on generating automations and tests, which are core Autopilot capabilities found in products like Studio, Studio Web, and Test Manager.
* Information about the Autopilot chat experience, available in two forms:
* Autopilot chat, integrated into various UiPath products
* Autopilot for Everyone, which is available exclusively in UiPath Assistant
* Guidance on managing and interacting with Autopilot features across different UiPath products.
* Information on Autopilot plugins and integrations with other platforms.
About Autopilot release notes:
* Autopilot own release notes focus on developments in the chat interface, Autopilot for Everyone, and Autopilot integrations.
* For Autopilot improvements in specific products, such as Studio or Test Manager, refer to that product release notes.
